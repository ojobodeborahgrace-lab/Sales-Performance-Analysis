# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes a sales dataset of over 2,000 transactions to uncover key business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

An interactive dashboard was built in Microsoft Excel to track performance across products, regions, and sales representatives.

---

##  Business Objectives

The goal of this analysis was to:

* Identify top-performing products and sales representatives
* Analyze revenue and profit across regions
* Understand monthly sales trends
* Highlight opportunities for business growth

---

##  Dataset

The dataset contains the following fields:

* Order Date
* Region & City
* Customer Type
* Sales Channel
* Product & Category
* Unit Price & Cost Price
* Quantity Sold
* Sales Representative

---

##  Tools & Skills

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Cleaning
* Data Analysis
* Data Visualization
* Dashboard Design

---

##  Key Metrics

The following metrics were created:

* **Revenue** = Unit Price × Quantity
* **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)** = Cost Price × Quantity
* **Profit** = Revenue − COGS
* **Total Transactions** = Count of sales records
* **Monthly Revenue Trends**

---

##  Dashboard Features

The Excel dashboard includes:

* KPI Cards (Revenue, Profit, COGS, Total Transactions)
* Monthly Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
* Revenue by Region (Bar Chart)
* Top Performing Products
* Best Sales Representatives
* Interactive Slicers (Region & Product Category)

---

##  Key Insights

* A small number of products generate the majority of revenue
* Revenue is concentrated in one region, indicating growth opportunities elsewhere
* Sales performance varies significantly across representatives
* Some high-revenue products have lower profit margins
* Sales trends show fluctuations across months (seasonality)

---

##  Recommendations

* Focus on high-margin products to improve profitability
* Develop strategies to boost performance in underperforming regions
* Provide training or incentives for low-performing sales reps
* Optimize inventory planning based on sales trends
